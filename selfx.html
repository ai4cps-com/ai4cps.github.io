What does AI4CPS stand for?

The term cyber-physical system (CPS) refers to the network of interconnected devices that intensively communicate in order to achieve a common goal. This is typical for production systems of today. AI4CPS refers to application of artificial intelligence (AI) to CPS use cases in order to improve performance of system's.

What are the performance indicators?

It could be throughput measured as a number of produced items per hour or energy savings. Perhaps waste reduction or number of person hours. Perhaps a number of some operations or percentage of machine's idle time. Or percentage of defect products. Or continuous measure of product quality such as distance or similar.

How can you benefit from AI?

Running a production requires many decisions to be made. For example, when to perform some operation, where to do it, should you replace some device due to failure, what material resources should you use, on how many parallel machines should you perform some process etc. AI can help in giving optimal or near-optimal answers to these questions with respect to your performance indicators.
